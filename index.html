How KiwiFix works

Whether you need a job done or you're the one doing it, getting started only takes a few minutes.

Post a job and get matched with trusted local tradies — no calling around, no guesswork.

1

2

3

Compare ratings and reviews from other Kiwis, then pick who you'd like to do the job.

4
Job done, sorted

Your tradie completes the work and you pay securely through KiwiFix — simple as that.

About KiwiFix

Kiwis supporting Kiwis — that's what KiwiFix is all about.

KiwiFix connects New Zealanders who need a job done with trusted, verified local tradies — from a leaky tap to a full renovation. We built KiwiFix because finding a reliable tradie shouldn't mean endless phone calls and waiting around for a call back, and growing a trade business shouldn't depend on luck or word of mouth alone.

Every tradie on KiwiFix is verified against their trade licence, so job posters can book with confidence, and every job posted is matched to the right local trades so tradies spend less time chasing leads and more time doing the work they're good at.
